# Impactly Demo Video Script

**Target Duration**: ~1 minute 30 seconds
**Speakers**: Speaker 1 (Parikshit) and Speaker 2 (Teammate / Vetty)

---

### **Scene 1: Introduction & Registration (0:00 - 0:15)**

**Speaker 1 (Parikshit):**
"Hello everyone, welcome to the official demo of Impactly! Our goal is to connect individuals with community service opportunities while gamifying the volunteer experience. Today, my teammate and I will walk you through the core workflow of the app."

**Speaker 2 (Teammate):**
"Starting off, I'm going to create a brand new account as a first-time user. The clean onboarding process asks for my basic details and lets me select community causes I genuinely care about—like the Environment or Art—so my feed can be properly personalized right from the start."

---

### **Scene 2: Creating an Event (0:15 - 0:35)**

**Speaker 1 (Parikshit):**
"Once on the home screen, you'll see a clean, categorized dashboard of ongoing events. Since we firmly believe in community-driven action, any registered user can step up to organize an event."

**Speaker 2 (Teammate):**
"Here, I'm creating the 'Ujjain Shipra Cleaning Drive 2.0'. I'll simply upload an appealing banner image, set the time and location, and allocate the Impact Points reward for the volunteers who show up."

---

### **Scene 3: Networking & Communication (0:35 - 0:55)**

**Speaker 1 (Parikshit):**
"Now, let's switch over to my account. When I log in, you can see I already have an established profile sitting at 200 Impact Points. The first thing I'll do is check my notifications panel, where everything is neatly organized. I see a new friend request from my teammate, which I can quickly accept."

**Speaker 2 (Teammate):**
"Impactly isn't just a bulletin board for events; it's a social network. You can search for users, visit their profiles, and interact. Now that we're connected as friends, we can seamlessly coordinate!"

---

### **Scene 4: Feed Engagement & Leaderboard (0:55 - 1:10)**

**Speaker 1 (Parikshit):**
"Next, I'll navigate to our shared event feed and reply to my teammate's recent post. We've built in a full comment system to keep volunteers continuously engaged and informed."

**Speaker 2 (Teammate):**
"And to keep motivation high, we've implemented a scalable Leaderboard. Users can immediately check where they rank against the entire community based on their verified volunteer hours."

---

### **Scene 5: Tracking Profile & Conclusion (1:10 - 1:30)**

**Speaker 1 (Parikshit):**
"Finally, we'll head over to my personal Profile. Here, I have complete visibility over my gamified stats, my newly accepted friends, and a private 'Events' tab where I can effortlessly track the events I have signed up for—like the Robotics Club Workshop."

**Speaker 2 (Teammate):**
"And that completes our core loop! From signing up and hosting an event, to networking, commenting, and tracking Impact Points. Thank you for watching!"
