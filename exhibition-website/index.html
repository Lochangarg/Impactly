The Challenge

The Problem We're Solving

Despite growing social awareness among youth, a significant disconnect exists between the desire to volunteer and the ability to take meaningful action.

60%

Hard to Find Events

Students struggle to find volunteer opportunities aligned with their interests, location, and schedule.

55%

Scattered Information

Event info is fragmented across Instagram, WhatsApp groups, notice boards — no single source of truth.

35%

Lack of Trust

Users distrust authenticity of events found through informal social media channels.

95%

Missed Opportunities

Nearly all respondents reported missing at least one volunteer opportunity due to insufficient information.

💡
The Gap: 80%+ of students use Instagram & WhatsApp for event discovery — but these channels lack filtering, event management, progress tracking, and recognition systems. Dedicated platforms like Unstop aren't localised for Indian university students.
Our Answer

Introducing Impactly

A dedicated, mobile-first platform with smart event filtering, gamified engagement, a community feed, and a trust-building social network — all tailored for Indian university students.

What Sets Us Apart

Our Unique Edge

01

Evidence-Driven Design

Every feature was validated through primary research (20-person survey) and 7 academic papers. Not assumptions — data.

02

Indian University Context

Unlike Unstop or VolunteerMatch, Impactly is built specifically for Indian college students — Hindi support, local categories, and campus-centric events.

03

Complete Ecosystem

Not just event listing — a full social-impact ecosystem with feed, messaging, friendships, gamification, and leaderboard in one app.

04

Real-Time Architecture

Built on Supabase Realtime subscriptions — live notifications, instant messaging, and reactive state updates without polling.

05

Row-Level Security

Every database table has RLS policies. Users can only access their own data. Privacy isn't an afterthought — it's structural.

06

Cross-Platform, Single Codebase

One Flutter codebase runs on Android, iOS, Web, and Windows — consistent premium experience everywhere.

Deep Dive

Key Features

A comprehensive look at what makes Impactly a complete social-impact platform.

📅

Event Discovery & Management

  • Browse by category — Education, Environment, Animals, Health, Music, Sports
  • Real-time search with text filtering
  • One-tap event joining with instant points
  • Create events with full details — title, description, category, location, date, reward points
  • Track all joined events in your private profile section
🏆

Gamification System

  • Impact Points for every meaningful action
  • Level progression system (every 500 points)
  • Global Leaderboard with Top 3 podium display (🥇🥈🥉)
  • Healthy competition driving sustained engagement
📰

Community Feed

  • Share volunteer experiences with photos, text, and event tags
  • Like ❤️ and Comment 💬 on community posts
  • Premium card-based layout inspired by modern social platforms
  • On-the-fly content translation for Hindi users
🔐

Authentication & Security

  • Email/Password auth via Supabase Auth
  • Forgot/Reset password flow with deep linking
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) on all database tables
  • Profile privacy toggle — control who sees your activity
  • Secure environment variable management via .env
🌐

Accessibility & UX

  • Full bilingual support — English & Hindi (100% coverage)
  • Dark mode with deep charcoal palette (#111827)
  • Material 3 design language with Inter typography
  • Persistent preferences (language, theme) via SharedPreferences
  • OTA update checker for seamless version management
